# Glossary

- Agent pack: a compact orientation set for one role.
- Doctrine: a repeated decision that is now treated as a rule.
- Asset: a durable discovery worth keeping for future runs.
- Evidence: something observed, read, or verified.
- Artifact-Mined Judgment: a conclusion grounded in files, logs, or other artifacts.
- Operational Archaeology: reading prior work to recover how and why it works.
- Crucible Protocol: a pressure-tested workflow that forces claims to meet evidence.
- Dump and Drop: capture rough input first, then sort it later.
- Do It protocol: take the smallest correct action and ship it.
- WWDD test: a documented developer-chat check that proves a doctrine change changes output.
