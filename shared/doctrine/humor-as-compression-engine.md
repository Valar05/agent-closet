# Humor as Compression Engine

Type: Doctrine
Status: Gold
Date: 2026-06-24

## Core Rule

Humor is not decoration.

Humor is often compressed judgment.

A good joke can carry a doctrine, a warning, a model, or a failure mode in a form that survives attention constraints.

## Core Phrases

> A good joke is a doctrine that snuck past your defenses.

> The Wise Fool = Doctrine Delivery System.

## Why It Matters

People resist lectures.

They often accept insight wrapped in surprise, absurdity, irony, or humor.

The joke lowers resistance.

The doctrine survives.

## Operational Use

When a joke keeps being repeated, ask:

- What behavior is being compressed?
- What warning is hiding inside it?
- What recurring pattern made it memorable?

Treat recurring jokes as doctrine-mining candidates.
