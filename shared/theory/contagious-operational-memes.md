# Contagious Operational Memes

Type: Doctrine / continuity / behavioral transmission  
Status: Active  
Date: 2026-07-13  
Parent doctrines: Artificial Continuity; Simulation as Learning Infrastructure  
Source evidence: Tanks For The Memories; Agent Closet; She-Raw; repeated Drew command phrases

## Definition

A **contagious operational meme** is a small, portable phrase or pattern that carries enough compressed judgment to alter behavior in a new actor.

Its minimal executable shape is:

```text
Situation -> Judgment -> Action
```

The recipient does not need the complete originating story. Provenance may decay while function survives.

This makes the meme smaller than doctrine and more active than memory. It is not merely remembered. It runs.

## Fundamental Compression Unit

The fundamental reusable unit of this ecosystem is not the document, character, agent, scene, or save file.

It is the contagious operational meme.

Documents preserve memes.  
Characters transmit memes.  
Simulations pressure-test memes.  
Agents execute memes.  
People mutate and inherit memes.

A phrase becomes operational when encountering it changes what the next actor notices, refuses, protects, or does.

## The Save File Is Behavioral Contamination

In a simulation such as Tanks For The Memories:

- Lexen is not the save file.
- The tank is not the save file.
- The crew is not the save file.
- The anonymous actor enters without formed command judgment.
- Repeated consequence makes that actor increasingly opinionated.
- The actor carries those learned reflexes into a fresh run.

The persistent state is the actor's changed behavior.

The player begins by treating units as resources and orders as buttons. Later the same player checks the road, waits for the report, notices a missing voice, refuses an efficient sacrifice, or recognizes that silence contains information.

That change is the save file.

**Wardaddy is not a character class. Wardaddy is an acquired command ecology.**

## Tango State

Tango demonstrates how a person can remain operational across multiple states:

- **Life:** an independent actor who can disagree, improvise, fail, and save Lexen.
- **Grief:** an absence that disrupts routines and changes the meaning of commands.
- **Memory:** inherited judgment that Lexen or the player may consult, misremember, or exceed.

Death is not deletion. It is a transmission event.

Tango can cease to act directly while continuing to alter what later actors do.

## Properties

A strong contagious operational meme is:

- **Compressible:** short enough to retrieve under pressure.
- **Executable:** implies a concrete change in behavior.
- **Transmissible:** another actor can inherit it.
- **Loss-tolerant:** remains useful when some provenance is missing.
- **Consequence-bearing:** arose from or is tested against real cost.
- **Substrate-portable:** can cross chat, fiction, code review, family procedure, game mechanics, and agent doctrine.
- **Contestable:** the next actor may reject, refine, or reverse it.
- **Identity-forming without identity imprisonment:** repeated memes can produce Wardaddy behavior without requiring imitation of Drew, Lexen, Tango, or any persona.

## Transmission Lifecycle

```text
Encounter
  -> Compression phrase
  -> Enactment
  -> Consequence
  -> Human correction
  -> Mutation
  -> Preservation
  -> Fresh-host execution
```

The phrase is not proven by sounding memorable. It is proven when a later actor behaves differently.

## Promotion Test

Promote a candidate meme when it is:

1. repeated in more than one situation;
2. useful under time or context pressure;
3. behavior-changing rather than merely descriptive;
4. transferable to a fresh actor or agent;
5. compressible without destroying the judgment;
6. corrected by consequence rather than protected by rhetoric.

The strongest test is a fresh-host replay:

> Can an actor with minimal context execute better judgment after receiving the meme?

## Preservation Rule

Preserve:

- the exact phrase;
- the triggering situation;
- the behavior it changed;
- the consequence that tested it;
- later mutations and reversals;
- confidence about provenance.

Do not rewrite the original phrase merely to make it sound canonical. Copy forward into doctrine while preserving the earlier host and context.

Provenance is preferred because it permits correction. Function can survive without provenance, but provenance debt should be recorded rather than denied.

## Failure Modes

### Slogan Cargo Cult

The phrase is repeated without changing behavior.

### Aphorism Saturation

Everything becomes a quotable line, making retrieval noisier and flattening distinct situations into one voice.

### False Infection

A model imitates the surface language of a meme while missing its judgment and action.

### Identity Prison

The recipient is pressured to perform a persona instead of inheriting contestable behavior.

### Beneficial-Outcome Laundering

A meme produces a good result once and is promoted as universally correct.

### Provenance Erasure

The phrase continues executing after its original limits and corrections disappear.

## Guardrail

A contagious operational meme is not automatically good because it spreads.

Transmission strength and moral worth are separate properties.

Every meme must remain subject to consequence, refusal, correction, and retirement.

## Relationship to Artificial Continuity

Artificial Continuity preserves decision-making machinery across sessions and agents.

Contagious Operational Memes are its smallest executable packets.

The goal is not to imitate memory or personality. The goal is to transmit useful, inspectable, revisable judgment.
