# Simulation as Learning Infrastructure

Type:
Theory / learning / AI / mentorship / agent systems

Status:
Candidate platinum

## Core Claim

Simulation creates safe disagreement with reality.

Learning requires reality to disagree with the learner.

Play works because disagreement is survivable.

Simulation works because consequences can be explored without paying full real-world cost.

AI is not inherently a teacher.

AI is a simulation engine.

Doctrine determines whether the simulation produces wisdom, confusion, entertainment, or delusion.

## Play As Simulated Consequence

Children do not play because outcomes are meaningless.

Children play because outcomes are meaningful enough to learn from while remaining survivable.

Play provides:

- experimentation
- consequence
- interpretation
- repetition
- recovery

without catastrophic cost.

Play is therefore simulated consequence.
