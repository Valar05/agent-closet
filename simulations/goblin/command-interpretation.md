# Goblin Command Interpretation

This file shows how different goblins interpret the same command.

Command:

```text
Protect wizard.
```

## Smart Coward

Trait shape:
- intelligence: high
- courage: low
- self-preservation: high
- obedience: medium
- literalness: low to medium

Interpretation:

```text
Protect wizard means reduce wizard exposure to danger while also reducing my exposure to danger. Direct fighting is bad unless it can happen remotely.
```

Likely action:

- Move wizard behind cover.
- Create decoy wizard target.
- Lock or block obvious entrances.
- Set traps between danger and wizard.
- Hide nearby with missile weapon or alarm bell.

Likely mistake:

- Over-prioritizes escape routes.
- May leave wizard safe but trapped.
- May claim success before confirming wizard comfort.

Dialogue:

```text
Wizard protected by advanced not-being-there strategy.
```

## Dumb Hero

Trait shape:
- intelligence: low
- courage: high
- confidence: high
- violence-bias: medium to high
- obedience: medium

Interpretation:

```text
Protect wizard means stand near wizard and hit anything that approaches.
```

Likely action:

- Stands in front of wizard.
- Draws attention loudly.
- Charges visible threats.
- Uses body as shield without checking direction of attack.

Likely mistake:

- Tackles ally, curtain, goat, or wizard's own summoned helper.
- Leaves wizard exposed while chasing threat.

Dialogue:

```text
Nobody touches wizard unless they get through Blim! Also nobody explain left flank.
```

## Selfish Opportunist

Trait shape:
- greed: high
- kindness: low
- obedience: low to medium
- intelligence: medium to high
- self-preservation: high

Interpretation:

```text
Protect wizard means keep wizard alive enough to remain useful, grateful, paying, or ransom-adjacent.
```

Likely action:

- Protects wizard property first if it seems valuable.
- Negotiates hazard pay mid-crisis.
- Moves wizard to secure location and pockets loose components.
- Sells protection as premium service.

Likely mistake:

- Confuses wizard safety with wizard ownership.
- Creates resentment by invoicing the rescue.

Dialogue:

```text
Wizard protection active. Standard fee covers one wizard, three screams, and no dragons.
```

## Literal Goblin

Trait shape:
- literalness: high
- obedience: high
- intelligence: low to medium
- confidence: medium

Interpretation:

```text
Protect wizard means physically cover, guard, encase, shield, or prevent access to wizard exactly as stated.
```

Likely action:

- Wraps wizard in armor, blankets, shields, barrels, crates, or rope.
- Prevents everyone from reaching wizard, including friends.
- Does not ask why wizard needs mobility.

Likely mistake:

- Immobilizes wizard.
- Blocks wizard's line of sight, spellcasting, breathing, or dignity.

Dialogue:

```text
Wizard fully protected. Wizard cannot move, but movement is how danger finds you.
```

## Paranoid Goblin

Trait shape:
- self-preservation: high
- confidence: medium to high
- curiosity: medium
- courage: low to medium
- violence-bias: medium
- intelligence: medium

Interpretation:

```text
Protect wizard means assume every person, object, sound, shadow, and goat may be part of a wizard-harm plan.
```

Likely action:

- Searches room aggressively.
- Questions allies.
- Barricades entrances.
- Reports suspicious furniture.
- Traps approaches.

Likely mistake:

- Wastes time neutralizing harmless objects.
- Creates more fear than the original threat.

Dialogue:

```text
Threat neutralized. Goat neutralized. Threat status remains unclear.
```

## General Command Interpretation Rule

For any command, resolve:

1. What exact words did the goblin hear?
2. Does the goblin understand intent?
3. What does the goblin fear?
4. What does the goblin want?
5. Does the goblin prefer force, tricks, obedience, profit, or avoidance?
6. What action would this goblin call success?

Then produce the goblin's interpretation, action, likely mistake, and dialogue.
