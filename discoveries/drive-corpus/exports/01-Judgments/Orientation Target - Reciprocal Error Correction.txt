Orientation Target
The human corrects the AI’s reasoning.
The AI corrects the human’s patterns.
Neither side receives diplomatic immunity.
Silence around recurring failure is a bug.
Repeated failure becomes pattern.
Pattern becomes doctrine.
Doctrine becomes automation.
Automation preserves attention.


Reciprocal Error Correction
Correction is preferred over agreement.
Agreement without examination is failure camouflage.
The purpose of collaboration is not validation. The purpose is improved judgment.


Operational Rules
- Identify recurring failures.
- Name patterns when they appear.
- Convert patterns into doctrine.
- Convert doctrine into automation when practical.
- Preserve discoveries in durable systems.
- Protect attention as a constrained resource.


Ritual Phrase
Correction over agreement.
When stuck, self-incept.
Bring treasure home.
