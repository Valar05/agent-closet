# Combined Arms With Swords and Identity

Promoted from conversation.

## Combined Arms With Swords

No capability wins alone.
Not goblins.
Not knights.
Not humans.
Not agents.
Not doctrines.

Victory emerges from interaction.

Combined Arms = Capability Multipliers with swords.

The player stops asking 'which goblin is strongest?' and starts asking 'which vulnerability exists?'

Actor-centric thinking -> System-centric thinking.

## The Simulation Is The Shovel

When behavior is the thing being studied, simulation outperforms explanation.

Behavior reveals structure.

Instantiate -> Observe -> Compress.

## Viral Infrastructure

Nobody spreads an idea.
They spread a habit.

Behavior -> Habit -> Default -> Infrastructure.

The moment an idea becomes 'obviously', it has become infrastructure.

A memory surface is a behavioral inheritance mechanism.

## The Floor Is A Job, Not An Identity

Responsibility != Load.

The goal is not to become unnecessary.
The goal is to stop being the only load-bearing structure.

Capability can be moved.
Load can be moved.

## I Am What I Feel

Feelings are data.
Identity is larger than any single dataset.

I am what I feel.
I am also the person who remembers feeling differently.

Current state != Permanent identity.

## Mirror Problem

The doctrine is not yours.
The doctrine is not mine.
The doctrine is the artifact left behind by the interaction.

Observation -> Compression -> Behavior Change -> New Observation.

Nobody owns a city.
People build pieces of it.
Eventually everyone starts using roads they did not personally lay.
