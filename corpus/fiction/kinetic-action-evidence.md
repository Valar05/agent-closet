## Provisional Patterns From Jumpy Bug Only

Do not promote yet.

1. **Threat becomes resource** — danger is often converted into food, weapon, shelter, evidence, or route knowledge.
2. **Companion as sensor/procedure** — Jumpy's clicks and webbing compress perception and response.
3. **Action as state conversion** — scenes rarely linger on motion unless the world state changes.
4. **Gross competence** — survival acts are disgusting, practical, and funny because they solve real problems.
5. **Denied option creates speed** — `no time to grab the spear` gives urgency and consequence immediately.
6. **One-word procedure** — established relationships allow compressed commands like `Web`.
