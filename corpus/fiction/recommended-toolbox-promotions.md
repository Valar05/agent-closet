# Recommended Promotions to the Canonical Writer's Toolbox

Status: recommended / not yet canonized
Source basis: cross-corpus pattern ledger
Purpose: shortlist the strongest reusable techniques for promotion

## 1. Promote "Protagonist as Routing Point"

This is the cleanest recurring structural move in the corpus.

Use when:

- the story needs a load-bearing human node
- the cast or system is fragmented
- responsibility has to feel earned

## 2. Promote "Maintenance as Dramatic Labor"

Repair, feeding, calibration, and stabilization repeatedly carry more weight than invention.

Use when:

- the world is already broken
- care should read as action
- you want the reader to feel survival as work

## 3. Promote "Systems Are Learned Through Contact"

The corpus consistently teaches by use before explanation.

Use when:

- the world has machinery, magic, interfaces, or protocols
- you want discovery to feel embodied
- you want exposition to stay subordinate to action

## 4. Promote "Food and Rest as State Transitions"

Meals and recovery are reliable scene-boundary signals.

Use when:

- one pressure state has ended
- the body needs to register that change
- you want recovery to matter without stopping momentum

## 5. Promote "Relationships Begin as Utility"

The corpus repeatedly turns functional cooperation into affection.

Use when:

- you want trust to feel earned
- the cast starts under pressure
- care should emerge from shared work

## 6. Promote "Progression Widens Decision Space"

This is a stronger progression rule than raw power scaling.

Use when:

- capability should increase agency
- you want upgrades to change play, not just numbers
- the protagonist needs more verbs, not just bigger damage

## 7. Promote "Failure Remains as Knowledge"

Persistent consequence is one of the corpus's most reliable meaning engines.

Use when:

- the world should feel durable
- learning should come at a cost
- you want the next scene to remember the last one

## 8. Promote "Comfort Is a Coercive System"

The corpus makes relief narratively dangerous in a productive way.

Use when:

- comfort can be used to control characters
- rest should be ambiguous
- you want temptation to be emotionally complex

## 9. Promote "Institutions Are Rational and Still Damaging"

The best institutional conflicts here are not stupid-villain conflicts.

Use when:

- authority has a defensible motive
- conflict should stay adult and credible
- the harm should come from optimization mismatch

## 10. Promote "Being Witnessed Restores Personhood"

Recognition is one of the corpus's cleanest rescue mechanisms.

Use when:

- the protagonist has been rendered illegible
- isolation is part of the premise
- you want communication to function as rescue, not ornament
