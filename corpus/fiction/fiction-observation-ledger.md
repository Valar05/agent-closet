## Status Terms

- **Observed:** Found in at least one source or one mining pass; not yet independently confirmed across enough canonical works.
- **Repeated:** Confirmed across multiple independent works or scenes.
- **Needs corroboration:** Promising pattern, but vulnerable to prior-doctrine bias or insufficient evidence.
- **Promoted:** Elevated into doctrine/theory only after repository evidence spans multiple canonical works.
