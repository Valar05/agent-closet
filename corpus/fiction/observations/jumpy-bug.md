Evidence:
- Opening locust / buzzing sequence.
- Jumpy’s mandible clicks speed up as danger increases, and Dylan responds before the threat fully arrives.

Compiler relevance:

- System: Spider instinct and human response form a shared warning system.
- Consequence: The group survives by reacting earlier than the threat expects.

- Judgment: Dylan switches tools immediately and burns through the problem.
- System: Survival uses whatever is at hand, including alcohol and flame.
- Consequence: The flies flee and the meal becomes salvageable.
