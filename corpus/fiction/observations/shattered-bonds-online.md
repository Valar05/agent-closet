### SBO-001 — Identity is eroded by divided attention, then restored by the game.

Evidence:
- Dorian/Jess breakup framing in Chapter 1 and the beta invitation.
- Dorian loses Jess in part because he keeps splitting attention between the relationship and his code; the beta invitation then offers a new place to focus.
- What state changes: social failure turns into a hunger for immersion; the game becomes a route back to competence and purpose.

Compiler relevance:
- Image: A man staring at code while a breakup happens on a video call.
- Pressure: Work fixation and emotional neglect have already caused a rupture.
- Judgment: He keeps choosing systems over presence until the breakup lands.
- System: Relationship maintenance, remote work, and game obsession all compete for attention.
- Consequence: Dorian becomes isolated, then vulnerable to any structure that rewards focus.
- Next pressure: The beta becomes a substitute for lost social belonging.
