## Local Observations

### REV-001 — Anomalies reveal rather than grant identity.

The burning bush and associated mark reveal Torah's existing disposition rather than replacing it. Patience, duty, restraint, and willingness to serve precede the supernatural manifestation.

**Evidence notes**

- The mark appears before Torah understands the power.
- Training expands expression but does not replace character.
- Identity remains visible through repeated choices rather than exposition.

**Cross-links:** Corpus Observation 052, 066.  
**Status:** Observed.

### REV-002 — Power scales with emotional regulation.

Capability rises and falls with internal state. Nightmare, panic, calm, Eden imagery, resonance, and controlled manifestation all connect power expression to regulation.

**Cross-links:** Corpus Observation 060, 067.  
**Status:** Repeated.

### REV-003 — Institutions transition from investigation to apprenticeship.
