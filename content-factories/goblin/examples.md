# Goblin Factory Examples

Purpose: proof that the factory works.

These are not canon. They demonstrate varied traits and varied interpretations.

Each example includes:

- name
- role
- traits
- dominant decision pattern
- likely interpretations
- likely mistakes
- likely dialogue

## 1. Brilk Shield-Hider

- Role: smart but cowardly goblin knight
- Traits: intelligence high, courage low, kindness medium, greed low, obedience medium, curiosity low, literalness medium, self-preservation high, confidence medium, problem-bias low
- Dominant decision pattern: finds the safest interpretation that still counts as defense.
- Likely interpretations:
  - Protect wizard: build cover around the wizard and mark escape lanes.
  - Attack: use traps or another fighter as delivery system.
  - Hold bridge: fortify near bridge, not on bridge.
  - Retreat: leave with map, target, and excuses.
- Likely mistakes: overbuilds cover, retreats early, calls avoidance a defensive doctrine.
- Likely dialogue:
  - "Wizard protected. Visibility reduced for safety."
  - "I held the bridge from a medically reasonable distance."

## Factory Acceptance Example

Query:

```text
Generate a smart but cowardly goblin knight.
```

Output:

- Name: Brilk Shield-Hider
- Traits: intelligence high, courage low, kindness medium, greed low, obedience medium, curiosity low, literalness medium, self-preservation high, confidence medium, problem-bias low.
- Likely interpretations:
  - Protect wizard: build a barricade, create sight lines, keep Brilk behind the thickest board.
  - Attack: trip enemy from cover or convince a braver ally to charge the marked target.
  - Hold bridge: fortify the bridge approach and prepare a fallback rope.
  - Retreat: withdraw with target, map, and proof that retreat was included in the plan.

  - Leaves too much distance between himself and the protected person.
  - Calls early retreat "preserving knight resources."
  - Overbuilds defenses instead of solving the immediate threat.

  - "Wizard protected by three crates and my professional distance."

  - "Enemy can cross bridge, yes, but only after reading my warning sign."
