## Purpose

Track what would actually prove Drink Simulation / Sense Synthesis doctrine.

This matrix separates three evidence types:

- internal corpus evidence,
- prediction/build/observation/deviation logs,
- external mechanism research.

Do not promote from this matrix alone. Promotion needs behavior-changing experiment records.

## Evidence Status Key

- `seeded`: claim exists in scratch but lacks tests.
- `corpus-backed`: derivative internal corpus evidence exists.
- `experiment-needed`: needs logged build and deviation.
- `research-needed`: needs external mechanism support or falsification check.
- `promotion-ready`: enough evidence exists to move through Crucible review.
