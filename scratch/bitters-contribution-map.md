## Purpose

Map bitters as aromatic and structural modules rather than treating them as generic bitterness.

This file is intentionally conservative. It records existing scaffold hypotheses and leaves observed contribution fields blank until tasted in controlled builds.

## Rules

- Do not invent tasting notes.
- Separate hypothesis from observation.
- Record dose, carrier, temperature, carbonation, and time.
- Capture failure modes at low and high dose.
- Treat bitters label alcohol content honestly.

## Minimum Test Protocol

For each bottle, run at least two carriers before promoting a contribution claim:

1. Cold still water.
2. Cold carbonated water, if available.

Optional second-pass carriers:

- lightly salted water,
- acidulated water,
- black tea concentrate,
- small highball build.

For each test, record:

- bottle and exact product name,
- dose in drops/dashes,
- carrier volume,
- temperature,
- first aroma,
- first sip,
- midpoint,
- finish,
- aftertaste,
- failure threshold.
