## Purpose

Pre-register the first five Drink Simulation tests needed to exploit the Sense Synthesis proof scaffold.

These entries intentionally contain predictions and observation fields, but no claimed tasting results. Fill the observation and deviation sections only after the drink is actually built and tasted.

## Ledger Rules

- Predict before tasting.
- Change one major lever at a time when testing a claim.
- Capture failure modes even when the drink is good.
- Preserve deviation as the reusable rule candidate.
- Safety-sensitive hydration or fermentation notes stay scratch until reviewed.
