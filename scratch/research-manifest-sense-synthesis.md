## Purpose

Preserve the research plan that can help prove or falsify Drink Simulation / Sense Synthesis doctrine without promoting it prematurely.

This is a manifest for targeted evidence gathering, not a doctrine page and not a recipe archive.

## Ownership Split

Quartermaster owns preservation:

- keep research metadata and rights notes durable,
- keep raw chat exports and unlicensed full text out of the repo,
- maintain links from scratch artifacts to evidence,
- prevent accidental promotion into `shared/`.

Prospector owns ore value:

- select sources that test high-value claims,
- reject decorative sources that do not change confidence,
- mark falsification risk,
- identify whether the larger ore is `Drink Simulation` or parent `Perceptual Composition`.

## Proof Ladder

1. Internal corpus evidence shows the pattern recurs in Drew's work.
2. Experiment logs show the model predicts useful sensory outcomes.
3. External research shows the mechanisms are plausible and named.
4. Promotion happens only if the doctrine changes future agent behavior.

External research is support evidence. It does not replace prediction, build, observation, and deviation logs.

## Source Criteria

A source is worth adding when it can be mapped to at least one row in [Sense Synthesis Evidence Matrix](sense-synthesis-evidence-matrix.md).

Minimum metadata:

- title,
- stable URL or DOI when available,
- source type,
- claim tags,
- lever tags,
- mode tags,
- rights note,
- safety domain,
- status.
