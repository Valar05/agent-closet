# Prospector Observation Pass — Lighter Than Air

Status: Scratch / Observe, Record, Correlate only.

## Story Hooks
- Government conscription of Potentiates at manifestation.
- Camp Dominion as institutional pressure cooker.
- Hydrogen powers evolve from utility to combat to engineering.
- Healer ethics split (Sienna vs political healers).
- Child (Alexis) becomes mission-critical infrastructure.
- Hydrogen scoop failure creates long-term engineering objective.

## Decision Points
- Obey institution vs question institution.
- Win fights through brute force vs creative application.
- Heal completely vs ration healing as social control.
- Protect children vs optimize mission throughput.
- Solve symptoms vs repair underlying system.

## Power Taxonomy
- Atomics (Hydrogen observed).
- Healing.
- Stone/body enhancement.
- Speed.
- Gravity manipulation.
- Cognitors (engineering/intelligence).

## Pattern Observations
- Every power creates institutional consequences.
- Utility powers become strategic powers.
- Engineering repeatedly beats raw strength over time.
- Moral conflicts emerge from incentives, not villains.
- Institutions drift toward exploiting reliable individuals.
- Children repeatedly become load-bearing patches.

## Simulation Opportunities
- Persistent institutions with memory.
- Reputation by repeated choices.
- Engineering projects running alongside narrative.
- Characters pursuing independent goals.
- Multiple valid solutions rather than binary morality.
- World advances while player is absent.

## Candidate Agent Behaviors
- Observe before explaining.
- Let consequences teach doctrine.
- Discover metaphors through simulation rather than narration.
- Treat emergent dialogue as first-class artifacts for later mining.
